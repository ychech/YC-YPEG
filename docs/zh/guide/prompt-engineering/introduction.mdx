  <p className="yc-intro-clean__zhline">没有。</p>

  <p className="yc-intro-clean__enline">So you have to define it.</p>
  <p className="yc-intro-clean__zhline">所以你得定义。</p>

  <p className="yc-intro-clean__enline">And you have to define it clearly—that’s why prompts matter.</p>
  <p className="yc-intro-clean__zhline">你定义就得说清楚，这就是为什么必须要提示词的理由。</p>

  <p className="yc-intro-clean__zhline">一个本身就是集合，抽象的内容，是没办法具体的落实实现的，一个缺少步骤的方向、地图的行驶，道路必定是曲折的。</p>

  <p className="yc-intro-clean__enline">Once you cross that stage, the tool becomes powerful.</p>
  <p className="yc-intro-clean__zhline">但是如果渡过了这个阶段，那么反过来，你有一个很强的工具，一个员工，很强大的武器。</p>
